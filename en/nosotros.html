About Us Services Geospatial Technical Support

High-altitude connectivity,
mountain experts

From the Cordillera,
connecting the unreachable

Born in Barreal, Calingasta, we understand life in the mountains, where challenges exceed 5,000 meters. This experience drives us to provide quick and efficient solutions, adapted to extreme terrains. With offices in San Juan Capital, Calingasta, and Iglesia, we reach the most remote places in Argentina and the region, offering connectivity, monitoring, and technology for mining, energy, and industry.

We conduct terrain surveys, processing data to generate a strategic information base that engineers and specialists can use in their analyses and in making key decisions at each phase of the project.
