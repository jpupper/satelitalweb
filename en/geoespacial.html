Geospatial
Analysis

Photogrammetry

We conduct topographic surveys using drones, generating 3D maps and high-precision georeferenced aerial photographs, ideal for construction, mining, and territorial planning projects.

Thermography

We use drones equipped with thermal cameras to detect temperature anomalies in critical infrastructure, facilitating preventive maintenance tasks and energy diagnostics.

Magnetometry

Aerial magnetometry using drones for geophysical explorations and resource detection.

Bathymetry

We conduct bathymetric studies with drones, collecting precise data on the bottom profile and depth of lakes and reservoirs in mountainous areas.

LiDAR

We use LiDAR technology with drones to generate high-resolution terrain models in high mountain areas, allowing precise analysis of relief and vegetation, with applications in exploration and project planning.

Asset Control

We monitor critical assets with autonomous drones, capable of operating in extreme conditions and managing flight missions remotely, ensuring periodic inspections in hard-to-reach areas.

Data Processing

We offer analysis and processing of data collected by drones, transforming information into detailed reports and useful visualizations for decision making.
