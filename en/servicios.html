Services

Connectivity / Monitoring / IT Management

We offer fully autonomous connectivity, as well as the design, manufacturing, and provision of equipment for environmental, meteorological, and security monitoring.

Connectivity

Secure and high-speed WiFi networks for reliable access.

We provide connectivity solutions designed for high mountain environments, ensuring access to internet and stable communications in extreme conditions, using completely self-sufficient equipment, satellite links, and long-range wireless networks.

Monitoring

Monitoring improves efficiency, reduces risks, and ensures optimal functioning of systems and processes.

We specialize in asset control through fixed cameras and advanced remote monitoring systems, designed to reduce risks and optimize operations in real time.


Visual: We supervise critical areas in real time with state-of-the-art cameras, high-complexity systems, cloud recording, and remote administration. Everything works autonomously, powered by solar panels with secure storage.


Meteorological: We install stations with early warning systems to monitor weather in remote areas, with data accessible online.

IT Management

We optimize your infrastructure with robust and reliable solutions

Networks: Management of secure and high-performance networks in extreme environments, with preventive maintenance.


Service Management: Professional IT administration to ensure operational continuity of your critical systems.
